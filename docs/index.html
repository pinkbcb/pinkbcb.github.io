Turning Data To Insight, and Insight To Cash

About Me

In high school, I had to do a senior capstone project. Most people opted for doing service hours or an academic report. I, however, took the initiative and completed a data science project analyzing equity and equality in public school funding. This project shaped my passion for data science and opened my mind to programming, analytics, and the power of data to tell stories.

Because of that experience, I am now a student at Brigham Young University – Idaho majoring in Data Science. I’m committed to finding data-driven solutions to complex problems. Through the Data Science Society, I’ve gained valuable hands-on experience—most notably helping the local fire department optimize equipment purchasing, repairs, and retirements. Our analysis doubled their budget allocation and brought their equipment up to code. Projects like this deepen my understanding of how analytics can make real-world impact.

I’m eager to continue learning and collaborating within this fast-growing field. I’m passionate about transforming raw data into meaningful insight and helping organizations make smarter, more ethical, and more effective decisions.
